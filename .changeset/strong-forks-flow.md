Add a home page that opens to a gallery of all your diagrams, each with a live preview, plus search, sorting by last modified, and favorites — with your shared diagrams shown alongside your local ones. You can create a diagram from scratch or from a template (previewing each) without leaving the gallery, and because every diagram now has its own address you can cmd/ctrl-click to open several in new tabs and jump back to the gallery from the editor. The imprint and privacy pages are reachable from anywhere, with a clear way back to whatever you were working on.
