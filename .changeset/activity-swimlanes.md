Adds swimlanes (activity partitions) to activity diagrams. Drop a swimlane from the sidebar, then add, remove, rename, or reorder its lanes and switch between vertical columns and horizontal rows from its edit popover. Drag a lane separator to resize the lanes. Activity elements dropped into a swimlane become its children and move with it; place each element under the lane for the role or system that performs that step.
