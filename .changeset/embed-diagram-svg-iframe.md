Embed your diagrams anywhere. Drop one into a README, an issue, a pull request, or any page as a Markdown image — it renders inline and updates as you keep editing — or paste a ready-made iframe snippet. The share dialog gains an "Embed" panel with copy-paste code, and embedding needs no sign-in or tokens: the diagram link is the key, exactly like opening it in the editor. The Share dialog itself is cleaner too — share once to get a single link, then choose what people can do with it (edit, collaborate, or give feedback) without ever creating duplicate copies. A popular diagram stays fast no matter how many people view it.
