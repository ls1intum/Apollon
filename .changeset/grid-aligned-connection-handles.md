Connecting edges is now easier and more precise. Every node side offers its centre and corners as connection points — plus quarter points on longer sides — and the available points grow with the node and adapt to the zoom level. While you drag a connection, a magnetic grid appears on the target side so you can land an edge exactly where you want, snapped to the canvas grid. Existing diagrams keep all their connections.
