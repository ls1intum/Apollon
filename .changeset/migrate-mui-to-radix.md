Migrate UI components from MUI/Emotion to Radix UI primitives, removing the MUI and Emotion dependencies. Introduces new internal components (Button, IconButton, Select, Tooltip, Typography, TextField) built on Radix and Tailwind CSS, and refactors all popovers, toolbars, controls, and the minimap to use the new system.
